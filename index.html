Computer Graphics Dude
I am currently a research engineer at Meta AI.
I completed my PhD at Tel Aviv University, in the Computer Graphics lab led by Daniel Cohen-Or,
followed by a post-doc position at Laboratoire d'Informatique de l'École Polytechnique (LIX).

My research has focused on shape analysis and deep learning application to the 3D and 2D domains.
