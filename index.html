I am currently a software developer at DNEG, a visual effects company.
I completed my PhD in Tel Aviv University, under the supervision of Daniel Cohen-Or,
followed by a post-doc in Laboratoire d'Informatique (LIX) at École Polytechnique.

My research has focused on shape analysis, shape and image similarity, segmentation and shape matching.
