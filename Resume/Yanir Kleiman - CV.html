I am a post-doc in Laboratoire d'Informatique (LIX) at École Polytechnique near Paris.
Before that I was a PhD student in the Computer Graphics lab at Tel Aviv University.

My professional experience spans two years as a 3D artist in visual effects and animation projects, and over 10 years in software development as programmer and team leader.

My research has focused on shape analysis, shape and image similarity, segmentation and shape matching.
Recently I have taken an interest in Deep Learning. This work has not been published yet.
